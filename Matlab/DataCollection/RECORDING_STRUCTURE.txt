Each folder represents one different subject

Inside, there are 60 csv files, called p1 to p61

p1-p10 contain movement from origin to target A, without obstacle
p11-p20 contain movement from origin to target B, without obstacle
p21-p30 contain movement from origin to target C, without obstacle
p31-p40 contain movement from origin to target A, with obstacle
p41-p50 contain movement from origin to target B, with obstacle
p51-p60 contain movement from origin to target C, with obstacle
